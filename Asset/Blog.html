My First Post

My name is Elshaday Abebe I’m software engineering student currently studying at addis ababa university institute of technology. I was born in a small town called Yirgalem which is found in SNNP region of sidama in the year 1992 E.C . Learned at union school from the time I started education up on grade 8. In grade 8 National examination I had scored a nice result and then I moved to yirgalem high school and completed grade 9. There next I came to a town called hawassa and joined Ethioparent’s school I studied there from grade 10 up to 12. After I had taken grade 10National examination I joined Natural science because of my strong interest to study Engineering especially software engineering. After I had completed grade 12 with good result. I joined Addis Ababa university to study engineering. Then, at the end of 1st semester of pre engineering she choosen rise 1st choice software engineering and finally joined it.
